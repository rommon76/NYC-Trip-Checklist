NYC-specific tips

▾
  • Tipping is expected. 18–20% sit-down restaurants (sometimes added automatically — check the bill), $1–2 per drink at bars, $1–2 per bag for hotel porters, 15–20% for taxis/Uber, $2–5 per night for housekeeping (left daily).
  • Transit — use OMNY, not MetroCard. Just tap your contactless card or phone (Apple Pay / Google Pay) at the turnstile. Fare capping kicks in after 12 rides in a 7-day window (effectively free unlimited).
  • Safety: Very safe in tourist areas but stay aware — don't leave bags hanging on chair backs, be cautious on late-night empty subway platforms, ride the front carriages near the conductor after dark.
  • Jaywalking is a way of life — but cars turn on green lights. Always check.
  • Sales tax (~8.875%) is added at the register, not shown on price tags.
  • Tap water is excellent — refill your bottle everywhere.
  • Time difference: NYC is 14 hours behind Sydney during NYC's daylight saving.
